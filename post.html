Current Workout Split

This is the workout split I believe will result in the most muscle gain for anyone willing to try it.

Posted by Chase Cumming on May 12, 2023

Chest/Tris

Incline Dumbbell Press for Upper Chest: 4 Sets @8-12 reps

Seated Upper Chest Press: 4 sets @8-12 reps

Overhead Tricep Extensions with Rope: 4 sets @12-16 reps

Superset with Low Tricep Extensions using Rope

Cable/Machine Fly's for Lower Chest: 4 sets @8-12 reps

Bis/Delts

Standing Bicep Curls with Easy Bar: 4 Sets @8-12 reps

Lateral Raises with Cables: 4 sets @8-12 reps

Front Dumbbell Raises: 4 sets @8-12 reps

Seated Bicep Curls with Neutral Grip: 4 sets @8-12 Reps

Back

Lat Pull-downs: 4 sets @8-12 reps

Seated Rows: 4 sets @8-12 reps

Lat-Pullover with Rope/Straight Bar: 4 sets @8-12 reps

Rear Delt Cable Extensions: 4 sets @8-12 reps

Legs

Back Squat with No Lockout: 4 sets @8-12 reps

Bulgarian Split Squats: 4 sets @10-12 reps

Dumbbell RDL's: 4 sets @10-12 reps

Quad Extensions: 4 sets @10-12 reps

Lying Down Hamstring Curls: 4 sets @10-12 reps

Seated/Standing Calf Raises: 7 sets @15 reps (hold at top)
